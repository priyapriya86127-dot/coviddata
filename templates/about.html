About COVID-19

What this application is and how to use it.

Purpose

This dashboard is a lightweight portal that turns your Excel workbook into a clean visual overview: key metrics, trends, vaccination progress, map view, and curated updates.

How it works

  • Data is loaded from covid19.xlsx on the server.
  • Charts are rendered in the browser using Chart.js and Plotly.
  • Pages are protected: you must log in to view the dashboard.

Quick links

Open Statistics, Map, Trends, Vaccination, and News from the sidebar.
